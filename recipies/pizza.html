Pizza

Description

Whether you're craving a classic Margherita or a loaded meat-lover's feast, nothing beats the magic of a perfectly baked pizza. This recipe focuses on a golden, chewy crust, a zesty herb-infused tomato sauce, and that iconic cheese pull we all live for. Its a versatile crowd-pleaser that brings the authentic taste of an Italian pizzeria right into your home kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Bread Flour
  • Warm Water
  • Instant Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Cheese
  • vegetables for topings (of choice)

Steps

  1. Activate and Mix
  2. Knead & Rise
  3. Shape the crust
  4. Top it off
  5. Bake and serve
